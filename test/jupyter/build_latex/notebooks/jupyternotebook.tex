The Wasserstein Distance for 1D distributions can be obtained by:
\begin{displaymath}
	\int_0^1 |C_\alpha^{-1}(r) - C_\beta^{-1}(r)|^p dr \\
	\int_0^1 |C_\alpha^{-1}(r) - C_\beta^{-1}(r)|^p dr
\end{displaymath}
